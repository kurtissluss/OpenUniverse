Hi, I'm Kurtis. It's nice to meet you.

Open Universe is repository of my ideas and work from the practical to the ridculous. I've spent so much of my life jumping from one idea to the next without building the required skills to unapologetically execute on them. My goal is that Open Universe serves as both a structure for my work and a guide for those taking a similar path. Everything here is free for you to use at your leisure.

A Bit About Me

  • I grew up in a small rural suburb outside of Indianapolis, IN where there wasn't much to do.
  • My upbringing has had nothing remarkable to lend myself to a career as a hacker, maker, or entreprenuer.
  • Slogging through college at Purdue University I earned B.S. degrees in Chemical Engineering and Chemistry, concurrently.
  • One field I naturraly excelled in was organic chemistry; this kicked off my life long crusade to learn more.
  • Brightlamp was a company started by me and a few friends in our last year at college.
  • I'm generally curious.
